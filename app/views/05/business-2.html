What is a Level 3 Apprenticeship?

Apprenticeships are real jobs that allow you to earn a wage while you learn. You'll work alongside experienced staff to learn on the job.

Intermediate (level 2) apprenticeships are equivalent to GCSEs. Advanced (level 3) apprenticeships are equivalent to A Levels or T Levels.
