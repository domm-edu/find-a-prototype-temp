Talk it through

It can be helpful to talk to someone, like a parent or teacher, about your choice. You can also talk to the National Careers Service (NCS) for free.

Check it out

You could attend an open day with the course provider if one is available. This can help you check how you'd get there and whether you like the facilities.

If you have a disability or any additional needs you could also find out about what extra support you could get.

You might even be able to talk to some students to get their views.

Apply

Apply for the course if you think it's right for you. You could ask someone to help with this if necessary.

Decide what to do before the course starts

If you're going to have a gap before the course or apprenticeship starts, there are loads of things you could do to top up your skills and put your time to good use.
