If you've been out of education or training for a while - for whatever reason - it might feel difficult to get back into it again.

There are loads of things you can do, though, that could:

  • grow your skills
  • increase your confidence
  • prepare you for sixth form or college

Even if you've been accepted for a course or apprenticeship, you might want to consider some of these opportunities to help use your time constructively before you start.

Short courses

Organisations that provide short courses for young people include:

  • King's Trust - free courses to build your confidence, get a job or start your business
  • Preparing for your future - free online course from Youth Employment UK for 14 to 16 year olds to build skills and careers confidence
  • Coursera - online short courses, many of which are free, in a variety of subjects
