It's common to feel a bit anxious about your next steps. But there's lots of support available.

Finding someone to chat to

Even if you think you know what you want to do, chatting it through with someone else can give you a different perspective.

You could talk to a parent, carer, teacher or careers advisor. Pick someone you trust and who will give you honest, helpful feedback on your ideas.

You can also talk to the National Careers Service (NCS) for free.

Mental health support

Whatever you're feeling, don't cope with it alone.

Childline has great resources for anyone up to the age of 18. You can talk to someone by phone, email or webchat. You can also use their message boards to chat safely and anonymously with other young people.
