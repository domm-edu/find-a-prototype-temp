Course overview

Are you caring, patient, observant and logical?

Do you want to make a difference and have a rewarding career supporting others?

Then this level 3 course is the course for you!

This is a full time, 2-year study programme, which covers a range of health and social care topics, which are assessed internally and externally. Learners will prepare for a health-related career with this Health and Social Care course, equivalent to A Levels.

It will give you the opportunity to develop your academic and writing skills and apply your knowledge to a real-life setting during practical work experience. Some of the topics covered are:

  • Principles of Safe Practice in Health and Social Care
  • Anatomy and physiology for health and social care
  • Enquiries into current research in Health and Social care
  • Promoting public health
  • Human Lifespan Development
